Our Story

Built on Experience.
Finished in Stone.

For more than 20 years, Shree GuruKrupa has been involved in the granite and stone industry, supplying quality materials for homes, commercial spaces, and construction projects. With deep knowledge of the trade and strong relationships in the market, we help customers choose reliable materials that combine durability with elegant design.

Our physical store was established in November 2022 in Jayapura, Karnataka. Today, we provide granite, tiles, marble, Kota stone, Kadapa stone, and sanitary utilities such as sinks and toilets. In addition to supplying materials, we also offer skilled labour for granite and tile fitting, helping customers complete their projects smoothly.
